WHY IS EMOTION RECOGNITION IMPORTANT?

Emotions can get in the way or get you on the way.
Inevitably feelings play an important role no only in our relations with other peo-ple but also in the way we use computers. Affective computing is a domain that focuses on user emotions while he interacts with computers and applications. As emotional state of a person may influence concentration, task solving and decision making skills, affective computing vision is to make systems able to recognize and influence human emotions in order to enhance productivity and effectiveness of working with computers.
